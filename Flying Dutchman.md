[[Magnolian Pirates]] may argue that it is a ship wreck more than a vessel, and whilst many of them might dispute and argue one thought is unified. It's is an uneasy and unplesent sight to see while sailing the [[Magnolian Sea]]. The captain of this ship is the one they call [[Black Beard]]. He, and he alone is responsible for maning this and sometimes accompanying fleet of goast ships that can choose to emerge out of the water at any point. If one may find themselves in the displesure of withnesing this ship, their best option is to flee. Seeing as the ship is self-sustainable, trading equaly damaging blows with the Flying Dutchman, the other ship simply can't afford loosing it's number of crewmates.
