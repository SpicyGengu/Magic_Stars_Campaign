#Location #Yarrow_Dessert 
A small town in [[Yarrow Dessert]]. New home to [[Mining Town of Jerboa|Jerboa]] refugees. Famous for their tequila. 
# Post [[(Session 11) Greenleaf falls]]
They are now researching the [[Yarrow Madness]].
# [[Fayendel Greenleaf]] Shrine
The devout followers of [[Fayendel Greenleaf]]s teachings have made and devoted a shrine to him. The shrine is made of pure carved bone and animal hide. 
# Night of the Phoenix
Every year since the events of [[(Session 11) Greenleaf falls]] the people of Armadillo celebrate the great rebirth. On the same date the [[Mining Town of Jerboa|Jerboa]]n population arrived, every year they make a giant campfire and celebrate the founding fathers ([[Yalier Verdrak]], [[Sombra (Calaca Sinclair)]], [[Fayendel Greenleaf]] and [[Grimshot]]).
