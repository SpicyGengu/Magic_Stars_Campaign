[[Kastel]] Observatory is a place of science. It is the worlds main hub for any kind of star-based research. It is the birthplace of exclusively cutting edge technologies all designed to research star constellations and [[Star Fragment]]s. Countless scientists would give anything just to ponder the halls of this place, even just for a day.
# Aquarius
Aquarius is the biggest and sharpest telescope the observatory has. It is capable of rendering any space object it observes in 3D. The closer the object is the sharper the details. It can be used to "walk" on the moon without ever having to go there.
