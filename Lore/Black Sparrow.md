#Lore #Magnolian_Sea 
The black sparrow is the fastest ship known to man. Seeing it's sails while out at [[Magnolian Sea]] is a bad omen. Out-running this vessle is simply never an option. Commanding this ship is one [[Paloma Blanca]]. She is a fierce captain with an even fiercer crew. All self respecting [[Magnolian Pirates]] dream of one day serving this beauty. 
# [[Living City of Kali|Kali]] Swallow
The black sparrow didn't always go by this name. Back when she was first built she was given the name "[[Living City of Kali|Kali]] Swallow". The ship was given to [[Paloma Blanca]] as a gift from [[Living City of Kali]] to aid her in her trades with them.
