#Lore 
[[Nameless City]] has a relic called the apple. Some know it as the Adams Apple. Biting this relic allows one to redefine the concept of life and death exactly as they please. The apple itself is a physical anomaly. Trying to understand it always leads to more questions.
# History
[[Sombra de los Muertos]] was the last being to take a bite of the apple, and by that the last one to define life and death.
