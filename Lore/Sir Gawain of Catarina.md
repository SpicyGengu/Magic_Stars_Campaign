#Lore  #Magnolian_Sea 
Gawain was a legendary knight. The best swordsman in all of [[Magnolia]]. He's tales of slaying beasts and armies alike have since all become legends told through generations. Beside his swordsmanship, Gawain's many proficiencies included the pen. He wrote a book called [[A Toast to A Knigh's Promise]]. In this book he writes about the many paths his life has led him down. About his knighthood, the foes he has slain, his friends and the [[Star Tomb (Magnolia)]]. He often writes about his old friend [[Yhorm, King of the Damned|Yhorm]], and the promise he made him. Sadly he never got to keep his promise. He died trying to find [[Yhorm, King of the Damned]]. Now his corpse and his [[Dying#The Damned|damned]] spirit can be found on the [[Gawain's Island]].
# Gawain's Promise
Gawain and [[Yhorm, King of the Damned|Yhorm]] were old childhood friends who grew up together. When [[Yhorm, King of the Damned|Yhorm]] took on the responsibility of becoming King of [[Dying#The Damned|The Damned]], he made Gawain promise that, one day, he would be the one to slay him. Gawain swore on his life that there would come a day when he would relieve [[Yhorm, King of the Damned]] of his eternal duties and allow him to move on from this life, free of regrets.
