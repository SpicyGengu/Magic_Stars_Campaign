#Lore #Silbian_Mountain_Ranges/Kali 
Sinclair is a family of [[Kali Elites]]. In fact The Sinclair family was the founding family of [[Living City of Kali]]. They were the ones responsible for building Kalis foundation, and have been out most influential ever since. The Sinclair's are seemingly all gifted with a silver tongue.
