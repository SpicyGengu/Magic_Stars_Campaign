#Lore #Magnolian_Sea 
This is a book written by one [[Sir Gawain of Catarina]]. The book can be found at the [[Magnolian Library]]. In this book [[Sir Gawain of Catarina]] writes about the many paths his life has led him down. About his knighthood, the foes he has slain, his friends and the [[Star Tomb (Magnolia)]]. He often writes about his old friend [[Yhorm, King of the Damned|Yhorm]], and the promise he made him.
# Notes
- Following the books directions one will end up finding [[Gawain's Island]] and not the [[Star Tomb (Magnolia)]].
