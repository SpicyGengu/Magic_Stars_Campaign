#Recap #Lore 
"We have a deal.", these were the words that concluded the deal made between [[Tayanna Sinclair]], representative of [[Living City of Kali|Kali]], and [[Star Up]]. [[Star Up]] was to invest all their energy into finding the remains of [[Antikal (Jerboa)]]. In exchange [[Tayanna Sinclair]] would take care of the problem they now had with [[Arthur]]. Beginning their search immediately [[Yalier Verdrak]] went off to the [[Magnolian Slums]] to see what he could do to get the rest of his team off of the blacklist. While he was there he went to check up on [[Dropotito Shipolito]], who had closed his shop for an undisclosed number of days. Confused, he went to the nearest bar to ask around. There he saw a friendly face. [[Đorđe Balašević]] was drinking at the same bar. The two caught up and agreed to meet another time, a little more planed. On another note, [[Sombra (Calaca Sinclair)|Calaca]] had begun to develop quite a taste for human blood again. Her [[Blood Addiction]] had started to rekindle. She was a frequent customer at the [[Wine & Dine]]. This is also where she caught wind of a man they called [[The Elder]]. The next day [[Yalier Verdrak|Yalier]] went back to see if [[Dropotito Shipolito]]'s store was closed still. Luckily it wasn't. [[Dropotito Shipolito]] managed to get [[Star Up]] off the blacklist just as he promised. He also directed [[Yalier Verdrak|Yalier]] to [[Fabriccado's Workshop]] telling him that [[Ado Fabriccado]] might be the right man to talk to in this situation. [[Yalier Verdrak|Yalier]] went back to the rest of the gang to tell them the good news. Next step, talk to [[Ado Fabriccado]]. The line was long but they managed. [[Ado Fabriccado]] was quite helpful. He didn't exactly know where [[Antikal (Jerboa)]] were hiding, but he gave them a good place to look. Other than telling [[Star Up]] about the [[Abandoned Graveyard]] he also hinted at knowing something about [[Friendly's Captors]]. Before setting out for the graveyard our heroes moved [[Yalier Verdrak|Yalier]]'s workshop to their new residence and even got a chance to meet [[Theseus]] their "neighbor".
