#Lore #Recap 
Gathering everyone up our heroes took the caravan of [[Mining Town of Jerboa|Jerboa]]ns and aimed their corsairs for [[Armadillo]]. This was voted to be the favoured destination for the people. The journey long. It took a full moon to reach [[Armadillo]]. On their way there The gang had made it their goal not only to keep everyone safe, sane and fed, but also to teach and make the remaining people of [[Mining Town of Jerboa|Jerboa]] in the caravan independent. [[Fayendel Greenleaf]] lead the hunting and scouting expeditions. His followers grew very attached to him, even started following his every word. [[Grimshot]] was in charge of protecting the caravan. Teaching people how to properly use a gun, in order to defend themselves and others if needed. [[Yalier Verdrak]] established a group of builders, who were, together with him, addressed the task of maintaining the caravan wagons. Together with [[Sombra (Calaca Sinclair)|Calaca]] he would settle disputes when they occurred. This was not [[Sombra (Calaca Sinclair)|Calaca]]s only duty. She kept up the morale, and made sure to sooth the people psychologically. At the end of each day they would gather into 4 campfires, one for each group. [[Sombra (Calaca Sinclair)|Calaca]]s was clearly the most desired one. Reaching [[Armadillo]] the towns folk decided to throw a celebration. This time they united all of the campfires into one and celebrated. In the morning both the settlers and the locals, gave our heroes all the [[Star Fragment]] they could muster together with a bottle of tequila for each.
