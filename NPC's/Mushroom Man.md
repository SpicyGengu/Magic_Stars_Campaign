#NPC #Magnolian_Sea 
The Mushroom Man is a being that is stuck inside the [[Abandoned Graveyard#Mushroom Room|Mushroom Room]] in the [[Abandoned Graveyard]] of [[Magnolia]]. He only reviles himself after talking to [[Melany]]. He has the bell tool.
# Notes
- The mushroom man will be willing to part ways with his bell tool but for the right price. If one can't be provided he will ask for [[Melany]]'s amulet.
