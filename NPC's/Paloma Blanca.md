#NPC #Magnolian_Sea 
A name feared throughout the [[Magnolian Sea]], even by [[Magnolian Pirates]], Paloma is the captain of the [[Black Sparrow]]. While terrifying Paloma resolves most of her conflicts peacefully. Her cruel and ruthless image contribute strongly to her bargaining power. Intimidation over bloodshed is her ideology. But don't be fooled, push comes to shove, she is in no way afraid to spill some blood. Paloma doesn't believe in "men at sea". She believes they are nothing but bad luck. In fact she generally doesn't respect men of no power. "All that birth given high ground and you still manage to stoop!".
