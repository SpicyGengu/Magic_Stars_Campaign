#NPC #Magnolian_Sea 
A name feard throughout the [[Magnolian Sea]], even by [[Magnolian Pirates]], Paloma is the captain of the [[Black Sparrow]]. While terrefying Paloma resolves most of her conflicts peacefuly. Her crule and rutheles image contribute strongly to her bargaining power. Intimidation over bloodshed is her ideology. But don't be fooled, push comes to shove, she is in no way afraid to spill some blood. Paloma doesn't belive in "men at sea". She belives they are nothing but bad luck. In fact she generally doesn't respect men of no power. "All that birthgiven highground and you still manage to stoop!".
