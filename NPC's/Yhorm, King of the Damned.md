#NPC #Magnolian_Sea 
Yhorm is the giant responsible for withstanding the collective pain and suffering of everyone who is [[Dying#The Damned|damned]]. He sits in his throne burning in green fire for all eternity. Finding him is a feat not achievable by many. He is hidden inside the deepest layers of the [[Star Tomb (Magnolia)]]. Here he guards the [[Star Tomb]] monolith, yearning desperately for the day [[Sir Gawain of Catarina]] will come to fulfill his promise.
# Notes
- Weak to [[Storm Ruler]].
- Killing him will result in the [[Dying#The Damned|damned]] getting more aggressive.
