#NPC #Magnolian_Sea #Dead-Gone 
Melany is a specter who is stuck in a mirror inside the [[Abandoned Graveyard]]. She knows a lot about [[The Elder]]. She is in the possession of a big red amulet.
# Post [[(Session 12) A Time of Reflection]]
Melany found a true friend in [[Sombra (Calaca Sinclair)]], who helped her realise her life long, longing to see the stars with her own 2 eyes. This action resolved her desires. Melany was now able to let go and was no longer [[Dying#The Damned|Damned]]. Meeting [[Sombra de los Muertos]] who severed her from this existence, the mirror shattered, and Melany was no more. (This is a big + for [[Sombra (Calaca Sinclair)|Calaca]] in [[Sombra de los Muertos|Sombra]]'s book).
