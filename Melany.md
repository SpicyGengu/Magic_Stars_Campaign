#NPC #Magnolian_Sea 
Melany is a specter who is stuck in a mirror inside the [[Abandoned Graveyard]]. She knows a lot about [[The Elder]]. She is in the possession of a big red amulet.
# Notes
- Asking Melany about the bell inside the [[Abandoned Graveyard#Maze|Maze]] she will ask for a copper mushroom. When received she will direct the party to the [Mushroom Man]] inside the [[Abandoned Graveyard#Mushroom Room|Mushroom Room]].
